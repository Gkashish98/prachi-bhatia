OUR MISSION

Identification of poor exemplary students and Funding based support from different NGOs, organizations and donations if they want to provide. Choice Based selection of such students from data set. For instance,if the requirement is only limited to academics, they can refer to the website to fetch a list of top scorers say top 100 or top 200 students.

OUR VISION

There are 29 states in India. Each state has different cities and villages across India. There is no mechanism to identify bright students who are performing well in studies, sports or other activities. Web-portal can be designed to acquire data about such students and can be analyzed on different parameters. What Exact Problem is being solved? : Such identified students can be provided with extra resources or special attention can be given to their upbringing .

Our Impact

EduGo has helped schools in various areas to increase the number of facilities for differently abled students. Braille Software has been developed, provision for lifts, rails and rest rooms have been executed. Academic accommodations for students with disabilities are provided on a case-by-case basis. Schools now engage in positive efforts to assure facilities. ECPI facilities are designed to help handicapped students.

EduGo made it possible for all the NGOs and government officials to view the performance of students in the schools. This not only helped with the donations but also with the tracking of the growth and success rate of the child education. Our platform provides with the relevant information in the form of lists and graphs which can be analysed for the results.

EduGo implemented various extra curricular activities including dance, drama, sports, art in various schools. Funds have been provided to schools directly through our platform. Students have been motivated to give equal time to these activities along with their studies by giving them performance based rewards. This has helped in creating awareness amongst students.
