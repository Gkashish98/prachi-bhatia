Focus areas

  • Child trafficking and child abuse

    Children are very vulnerable when it comes to Domestic servitude. Often children are told that they will be offered excellent wages to work as a maid in middle-class homes, but they usually end up being severely underpaid and abused. Every year hundreds of thousands of girls are trafficked from rural India to work as maids. A root cause of commercial exploitation and child trafficking in India is due to lack of education, and the need to support their family.

  • Early or child marriage

    27% of girls in India are married before their 18th birthday. Child marriage is more common among poorer households, with many families marrying off their daughters to reduce their perceived economic burden. Girls are often married off at a younger age or during schooling because less dowry is expected for younger brides. Girls' education benefit her marital family, and educating daughters is therefore seen as less of a priority than educating sons.

  • Children with disabilities

    A large number of children with disabilities do not go to regular schools. While there has been a drop in enrolment of students with visual impairments, there has been a rise in those with multiple disabilities. The percentage of children attending schools is the lowest among those with multiple disabilities. The attitude of parents towards including children with disabilities into mainstream education is also crucial to accomplish the goal of education.

  • Improve in co-curricular activities

    Co-curricular activities are meant to bring social skills, intellectual skills, moral values, personality progress and character appeal in students. It includes athletics, cultural events, library activities, classroom activities, creative arts and meditation. It helps them sharpen their communication skills, public speaking, participation.They are good for improving students learning experience which improves their attendance at school.

  • Scholarships and rewards

    The objective is to provide financial assistance to the meritorious students. Getting a scholarship takes away all your financial concerns. Thus, it gives you more time to study, gain knowledge and secure better grades in school. You get ample time to look for opportunities for skill development and knowledge enhancement. These scholarships helps in empowering your academic and career goals by removing the financial barrier.
