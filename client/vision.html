OUR VISION

We believe in gathering and analysing the information. We support the government in the development, implementation and monitoring of the details and also provide the institutions with funding.

Our platform provides government officials and registered NGOs with the dashboard where a detailed analysis can be performed. It also helps with the visual data and an option for easy fundings.

We bring the partners together for a better future of the child education.EduGo leverages the financial support of NGOs,the private sector and philanthropy to strengthen education systems in developing countries.

Our strategy aims to mobilize more and better financing for education by attracting additional donors to education, seeking increased financing from traditional donors, and advocating for increased domestic funding of education.

EduGo adheres to high standards of transparency in regards to its policies, financing, and program funding. We regularly publish data compliant with the standards.

Our goal at this level is to strengthen education sector planning and policy implementation. We bring together all education partners in local education groups (LEGs), which enable them to contribute at all stages of the education planning cycle, from sector analysis to evaluation.

"EDU GO supports developing country governments to develop good quality education sector plans. Governments take the lead in planning and are accountable for delivery"
